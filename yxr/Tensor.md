## 内积

对应元素相乘并求和。

$$
\left< \mathcal{X}, \mathcal{Y} \right> = \sum \limits_{i_{1}=1}^{I_{1}}\sum \limits_{i_{2}=1}^{I_{2}} \dots \sum \limits_{i_{N}=1}^{I_{N}} x_{i_{1}i_{2} \dots i_{N}} y_{i_{1}i_{2} \dots i_{N}}
$$
